What are these tables?

These tables show the complete results (finishers and volunteers) for the specified week at this parkrun. It is automatically shown when you navigate to the results page, but can also be reached by clicking on Latest Results, or by picking a specific week from the Event History table. If you bookmark the Latest Results page in your browser, the bookmark will always take you to the most up to date results table. If you bookmark a results page reached via the Event History table, the bookmark will then always take you to that specific week's results.

The data

  • Position - the participant's overall position in the parkrun.
  • parkrunner - is the registered name of the parkrunner; clicking on the name takes you to that person's results page for this event.
  • Time - is the participant's recorded time - we record the overall time between the Run Director's start instruction and the finisher crossing the line.
  • Age Group - represents the age category of the participant on the day of the event; the first letter represents the major age categories (J)unior, (S)enior and (Veteran); the second letter represents gender (M)en and (W)omen; the remainder of the category represents a more specific age range. There are two further categories for wheelchair users; WWC and MWC represent Women and Men respectively.
  • Age
    Grade - Gives an indication of how well the parkrunner has done relative to their age and gender see the FAQ article for more information.
  • Gender - Male or Female
  • Group - group if registered.
  • PB - Personal Best
  • parkruns - Total number of finishes across parkruns of this type (5k or junior), and an indicator of membership of the various parkrun clubs.

Tables can be sorted and searched using the filters at the top of each table.
